<div><h3>Delhi Development Authority v Archana Khanna and others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>16 August 2016
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i> Kurian Joseph, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>Civil Appeal No. 8154 of 2016
</p><p>(Non-Reportable)
</p><p>1. Leave granted.
</p><p>2. It is not in dispute that in this appeal, the award is dated 07.01.1999 and that there was no stay operating and yet, possession has not been taken. Therefore, Section 24(2) of The <a href="/acts/Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013">Right to Fair Compensation and Transparency in Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013</a> has to apply and the acquisition is to be declared as lapsed.
</p><p>3. In that view of the matter, the appeal is dismissed.
</p><p>4. No costs.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
