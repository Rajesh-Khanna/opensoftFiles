<div><h3>Bharat Overseas Bank Limited v V.Raman and Anr
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>30 April 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>I.A. No.2 In C.A..Nos.457 & 458 of 1999
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  In both these appeals, the appellants and the contesting respondent have entered into a settlement. Copy of the settlement deed together with affidavits of the parties accepting the terms of the settlement deed have been filed. We take the settlement deed on record.
</p><p>2. In modification of the impugned orders, the appeals are disposed of in terms of the settlement as arrived at.
</p><p>The terms of the settlement shall form a part of the order. No costs.
</p><p>Appeals disposed of.
</p></div>
