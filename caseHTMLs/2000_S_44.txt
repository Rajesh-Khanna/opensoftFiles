<div><h3>Rajiv Sachdeva v S.B.I., Dhanbad and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>10 November 2000
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6294/1999
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>1.  In view of the recent judgment of this Court in United Bank of India vs. Abhijeet Tea Co. Ltd., we set aside the judgment of the High Court dated 25.9.98 and direct the Registrar of the High Court of Patna to transfer the counter claim filed by the petitioner, to the Tribunal constituted under the Recovery of Debts due to Banks and; Financial Institutions Act, 1993, to be tried along with the connected Title (Mortgage) Suit No.38//86.
</p><p>Appeal is disposed of accordingly.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of.
</p></div>
