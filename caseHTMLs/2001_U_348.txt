<div><h3>C.R. Spinners Private Limited v United India Insurance Company and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>31 August 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 5495 of 2001
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follow:
</p><p>1.  This appeal under Section 23 of the <a href="/acts/Consumer Protection Act, 1986">Consumer Protection Act, 1986</a>, arises out of the final judgment and order dated 2nd May, 2001 passed by the National Consumer Disputes Commission. The Commission dismissed the complaint by reason of facturn of issuance of receipt of an agreed amount in full and final settlement of the complainant claim arising out of a policy of insurance.
</p><p>2.  The record depicts that the complainant by his letter dated 30.8.1994 and voucher dated 30.8.1994 did accept the amount of Rs. 27, 41, 364/- (Rupees twenty seven lakh forty one thousand three hundred and sixty four only) in full and final settlement of the complainant claim arising under the policy issued by the first opposite party.
</p><p>3.  On the wake of the aforesaid, we are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order of the Commission hence this appeal is dismissed. No costs.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
</p></div>
