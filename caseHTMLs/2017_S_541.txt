<div><h3>Shyam Lal v State of Rajasthan
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>10 July 2017
</b><p>
</p><p>Criminal Appeal No(s). 1779/2014 (FOR Grant of Bail on IA 6247/2017 as per R/P Dated 08.05.2017 Only IA 6247/2017 IS to be Listed Before Hon'ble Court After Summer Vacations)
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows :
</p><p>CRL.M.P. NO.6247/2017 IN CRL.A. NO.1780/2014
</p><p>1. We have heard the learned counsels for the parties and perused the relevant materials.
</p><p>2. The accused appellant is in custody for over 13 years. The appeal being of the year 2014 is not likely to be heard in the near future unless expedited which course of action we are not inclined to order. Taking into account the period of custody suffered and the time that may be taken in disposing of the appeal we order for the release of the accused appellant on bail subject to the satisfaction of the learned trial Court in connection with Criminal Case No.12 of 2006 arising out of FIR No.181 of 2004, P.S. Jhotwara Jaipur.
</p><p>3. The learned trial Court is free to impose such conditions as may be considered necessary.
</p><p>4. The Crl. M.P. is disposed of in the above terms.
</p><p>Petition disposed of
