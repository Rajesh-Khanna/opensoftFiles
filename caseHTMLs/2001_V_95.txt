<div><h3>Vikram International, Gujarat v Commissioner of Customs, Kandla, Gujarat
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>21 August 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 5019 of 1999
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  It appears that the matter in issue stands covered by Basant Industries vs. Addl. Collector of Customs, Bombay as also by a later decision of this Court in Eicher Tractors Ltd., Haryana vs. Commissioner of Customs, Mumbai.
</p><p>2. In that view of the matter, these appeals succeed and the order of the Tribunal is set aside.
</p><p>Appeals allowed.
</p></div>
