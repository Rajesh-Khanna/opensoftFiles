<div><h3>Pritam Singh v Supdt. Canal Officer, Ferozepur and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>6 August 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.5759/2000
</p><p>The order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>The order made by the High Court is cryptic one.
</p><p>1.  It does not disclose that there is due application of mind of the Court to the facts arising in the matter before the decision is rendered.
</p><p>2. In the circumstances, we set aside the order made by the High Court and remit the matter to the High Court for fresh consideration in accordance with law.
</p><p>The appeal is allowed accordingly.
</p><p>Appeal allowed.
</p></div>
