<div><h3>Common Cause, A. Regd. Society v Union of India and another
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>12 September 2002
</b><p>
</p><p>Review Petition (C) No. 891 of 2002 in RP (C) No. 98 of 1997 in W.P. (C) No. 26 of 1995
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  After delivering of the judgment in a writ petition filed under Article 32, a review petition had been filed, and that review petition had been entertained and finally disposed of. The present application purports to be a review petition against the reviewed judgment.
</p><p>2. This application is wholly misconceived and there is no provision anywhere to file successive review petitions. The review petition is accordingly dismissed.
</p><p>Review dismissed.
</p></div>
