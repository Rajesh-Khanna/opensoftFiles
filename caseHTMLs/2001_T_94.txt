<div><h3>Union of India v Thakhat Singh and Anr
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>8 November 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>CIVIL APPEAL NO. 1032 OF 1999
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  It is conceded that this appeal stands covered by the decision of this Court in Sunder Vs. Union of India.
</p><p>2. In view of the above cited decision, we do not find any merit in this appeal. It is, accordingly, dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
