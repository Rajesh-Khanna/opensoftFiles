<div><h3>B. P. Sharma v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>10 April 1981
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i>The Judgment was delivered by: MISRA, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>Civil W.P. No. 3806 of 1980.
</p><p>1.  By this petition, the petitioner only wanted a similar stay order as was passed in the case of Ram Gopal Shukla (Civil appeal No. 282 of 1980) on April 23, 1980, in C. M. P. No. 1634 of 1980. That relief has already been granted to the petitioner, as the counsel for the petitioner states.
</p><p>2. In the circumstances, this writ petition has become infructuous and is accordingly dismissed.
</p><p>Petition dismissed.
