</p><b>26 September 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>CIVIL APPEAL NO. 3408/1998
</p><p>The Order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Learned counsel on both sides are agreed that this appeal is covered by the decision of this Court in C.A. Nos. 5740-5741/1997 - State of Haryana and Ors. Vs. Ravinder Kumar and Ors. and connected batch disposed of on 31.10.2000.
