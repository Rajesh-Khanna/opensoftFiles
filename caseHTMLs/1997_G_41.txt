<div><h3>Gobind Sugar Mills Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>16 July 1997
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 20 of 1985 and C.A. No. 123 of 1989
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Civil Appeal No. 123 of 1989: In this case the expenditure relates to the acquisition of a leasehold right for setting up of a sugar factory. The expenditure is clearly of a capital nature.
</p><p>2. The appeal is dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs Civil Appeal No. 20 of 1985: In view of the order passed in C. A. No. 123 of 1989, this appeal is dismissed.
</p><p>3. There shall be no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
