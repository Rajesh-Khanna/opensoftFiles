<div><h3>M.E. Sathidevi Amma v Special Tahsildar
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>12 February 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13187/2000
</p><p>The order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>Special leave granted.
</p><p>1.  Learned counsel for the appellant states that in the event of the Constitution Bench deciding the issue regarding interest and solatium in favour of the State, the appellant shall refund the interest on solatium merely on a demand being made by the State within four weeks of the said demand.
</p><p>2.  In this view of the matter, this appeal is allowed in the aforesaid terms.
</p><p>Appeal allowed.
</p></div>
