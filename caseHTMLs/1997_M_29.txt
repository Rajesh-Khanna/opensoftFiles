<div><h3>Additional Commissioner of Income Tax v M. Nagappa and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>23 July 1997
</b><p>
</p><p>C.As. Nos. 3108 to 3113 of 1980 (Appeals from the judgment and order Dt. 27 June 1980, of the Karnataka High Court in Writ Appeals Nos. 424 to 429 of 1974).
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  The judgment under appeal has already been referred to and overruled in the case of Ganesh Dass Sreeram v. ITO <a href="/case/1987 Indlaw SC 28790">1987 Indlaw SC 28790</a>.
</p><p>2. In that view of the matter, the judgment under appeal is set aside. The appeals are allowed. There will be no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeals allowed.
