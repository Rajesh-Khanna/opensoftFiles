<div><h3>Manisha and Others v Vijay Ojha
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>11 December 2000
</b><p>
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  The present transfer petition is for the transfer of Case No. 135/98 pending in the Family Court at Faizabad (U.P.) to the competent court at Delhi. Looking to the averments made in the petition and having gone through the counter affidavit, on the facts of this case, we feel it appropriate, that it is a fit case to be transferred to Delhi.
</p><p>Accordingly, we direct, the Case No. 135/98 titled Vijay Ojha Vs.Manisha and Ors. pending in the Family Court, Faizabad (U.P.) be transferred to the court at Delhi.
</p><p>2. The matter may be placed before the District Judge, Delhi Court to assign it to a competent court having such jurisdiction to try this case.
</p><p>3.  The record pertaining to this case may also be sent by the said court to the Delhi Court expeditiously. Accordingly, the petition is allowed. Cost on the parties.
</p><p>4. In view of the decision in T.P.(C)No. 336/1999 this transfer petition is allowed. Cost on the parties.
</p><p>Petition allowed.
</p></div>
