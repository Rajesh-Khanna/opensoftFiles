<div><h3>Pheru Singh Sharma v State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>30 July 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20662-20663/2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>1.  In view of the decision of this Court in Punjab National Bank. Vs. Kunj Bihar Mishra, these appeals are allowed.

</p><p>Appeals allowed.
