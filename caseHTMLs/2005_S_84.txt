<div><h3>Sanjiv Kumar v Om Prakash Chautala and another
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>21 February 2005
</b><p>
</p><p>Contempt Petition (C) No. 448 of 2004 in W.P. (Cri.) No. 93 of 2003, Cri. MPs Nos. 13517-18 of 2004 and 2322 of 2005
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  We would like to take up this matter for hearing in chambers on 7-3-2005.
</p><p>2.  In the meantime, it is directed that so far as the petitioner Sanjiv Kumar is concerned, he shall not be arrested and no prosecution against him shall be filed by CBI except by the leave of this Court.
