<div><h3>Transmission Corporation of Andhra Pradesh v Ch. Prabhakar and others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>29 September 2010
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 6131 of 2002
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  In view of the subsequent legislation, namely, <a href="/acts/Electricity Act, 2003">Electricity Act, 2003</a>, learned counsel appearing on behalf of the parties say that the matter has become academic.
</p><p>2. Accordingly, we need not answer the reference made to the Constitution Bench. Consequently, nothing survives in this appeal and the same is accordingly dismissed.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
