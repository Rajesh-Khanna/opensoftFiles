<div><h3>Commissioner Of Income Tax v Ganapati Panchayatan Sansthan Trust
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>1 November 2000
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. Nos.15425-15429 of 1996
</p><p>The Order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Having regard to the fact that the Revenue has accepted the position that is contested in these appeals and special leave petition in respect of several years other than the years in question here, we are not inclined to entertain the civil appeals and special leave petition.
</p><p>2.  The civil appeals and special leave petition are dismissed leaving the question of law open.
</p><p>No order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeals dismissed.
</p></div>
