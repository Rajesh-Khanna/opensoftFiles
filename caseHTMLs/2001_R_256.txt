<div><h3>Rashi Gangwani and Others v Shailesh Gangwani and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>13 August 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No.7142-7143/1999
</p><p>The order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Learned Arbitrator has filed an Award dated 24th August, 2000. The Award is based on a settlement arrived at between the parties during the arbitration proceedings.
</p><p>2. Learned counsel for the parties submit that in view of the provisions of Section 35 of Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, the Award made by the learned Arbitrator is final and binding on the parties. We record the statement of the learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>The appeals be consigned to record.
</p><p>Order accordingly.
