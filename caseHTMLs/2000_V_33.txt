<div><h3>V.V.Subba Rao and Others v Veereswaraswami
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>28 November 2000
</b><p>
</p><p>I.A.No.12-22 in C.A..No.3576-3586/2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  This Court passed an order on 10.7.2000 in Civil Appeal Nos.3576-3586 of 2000. These interlocutory applications have been filed for some corrections in the said order. For the words "Commissioner of Survey, Settlements and Land Records" the words "Commissioner of Land Revenue" shall stand substituted. In penultimate paragraph for the words "writ petition" the words "Civil Revision Petition" shall stand substituted.
</p><p>The I.As. are allowed accordingly.
</p><p>Application allowed.
