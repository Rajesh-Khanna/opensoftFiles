<div><h3>Commissioner of Wealth Tax v London Star Diamond Company (I) Private Limited
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>13 August 1999
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i>The Judgment was delivered by: B. N. KIRPAL, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>C.A. Nos. 4465 to 4467 of 1999
</p><p>Special leave granted.
</p><p>1.  After hearing learned senior counsel for the parties, in our opinion a question of law does arise. We, therefore, direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following question of law to the High Court.
</p><p>"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was right in law in holding that the value of the stock in trade does not require to be included in the net wealth for the purpose of determining the wealth tax liability of the assessee-company ?"
</p><p>The appeals stand disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
</p><p>Appeals disposed of.
