<div><h3>State (Cbi)/S. P. E v P. V. Narasimha Rao and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>16 December 1998
</b><p>
</p><p>Review Petition (Crl.) 2210-2227 of 1998 with Crl. Nos. 1207-22 of 1997, 186 and 187 of 1998
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows :
</p><p>1.  There is an inordinate delay of 179 days in filing these review petitions.
</p><p>2. The application seeking condo nation of delay is vague, indefinite and contains no reasonable or satisfactory explanation.
</p><p>3. That the delay has occurred because of "paucity of staff", is hardly an explanation for seeking condo nation of such an inordinate delay.
</p><p>The application seeking condo nation of delay is; therefore, rejected.
</p><p>4. Consequently, the review petitions shall stand dismissed, as barred by time.
</p><p>Petition dismissed
</p></div>
