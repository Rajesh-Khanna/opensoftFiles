<div><h3>Card Board Box Manufacturing Company v Collector of Central Excise, Calcutta
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>14 July 1994
</b><p>
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  We have today delivered judgment in Rollatainers Ltd. v. Union of India.
</p><p>2.  For the reasons and the conclusions reached by us in the said appeal, this civil appeal is dismissed and the order dated 27-3-1984 of the Central Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal, New Delhi is upheld. No costs.
</p><p>Appeal dismissed.
</p></div>
