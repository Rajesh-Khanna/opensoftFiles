<div><h3>State of Rajasthan v Nandlal Singh and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>13 January 1995
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 695 of 1995 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 7468 of 1993)
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Special leave granted.
</p><p>1.  We have today pronounced judgment in - State of Rajasthan v. Gopaldas. For the reasons and conclusions reached by us in Gopaldas case, we allow the appeal and set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court.
