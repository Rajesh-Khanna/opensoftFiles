<div><h3>United Phosphorous Ltd v Director General (Safeguards)
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>5  May  2000
</b><p>
</p><p>S.L.P. (Civil ) No. 6967 of 2000, Dt. 5 May 2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  We decline to invoke the jurisdiction under Article 136 of the Constitution in regard to what is only a recommendation.
</p><p>2. This, in our view, should also have governed the writ petition filed by the petitioners.
</p><p>The special leave petition is dismissed.
</p><p>Petition dismissed.
