<div><h3>Madhuri v Kurukshetra University
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>14 February 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Review Petition(Civil) Nos.140-141/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)Nos. 6422-6423/2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  There is an inordinate delay of 188 days in filing the review petitions which has not been explained satisfactorily.
</p><p>2.  Apart from this, we do not find any merit in the review petitions. Accordingly, the petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
</p><p>Petitions dismissed.
</p></div>
