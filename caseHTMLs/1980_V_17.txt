<div><h3>Vinod Kumar Shantilal Gosalia v Gangadhar and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>24 July 1980
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i>The Judgment was delivered by: FAZAL ALI, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>C.As. Nos. 1440-1443 (N) of 1970.
</p><p>After having heard counsel for the parties we reserved judgment.
</p><p>1. On going through the judgment of the Judicial Commissioner and the documents and after a careful consideration of the arguments of the parties, we find that these appeals involve a substantial question of law of great importance which likely to govern a number of cases arising out of mining leases in the present territory of Goa, Daman & Diu.
</p><p>2. We, therefore, direct that this case be placed before a larger Bench. Let these appeals be placed before the Hon'ble the Chief Justice for orders.
</p><p>Order Accordingly.
