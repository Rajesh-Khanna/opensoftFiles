<div><h3>Babita v Girdharilal Jiyalal Chaurasia
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>25 November 2008
</b><p>
</p><p>Transfer Petition (Civil) No. 136 of 2008
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows :
</p><p>1.  This is a petition filed by the petitioner-wife under Section 25 of the <a href="/acts/Code of Civil Procedure, 1908">Code of Civil Procedure, 1908</a> read with Order XXXVI-B of the Supreme Court Rules, 1966 seeking transfer of M.J. Petition No.163/2007 filed by the respondent-husband before the Family Court, District Azamgarh, U.P., to the Additional Chief Metropolitan Court, Bandra Mumbai, Maharashtra. Despite service of notice, no one has entered appearance for the respondent.
</p><p>2.  Considering the facts and circumstances stated in the petition, we direct that M.J. Petition No.163/2007 titled as Girdhari Lal vs. Smt. Babita pending before the Family Court, District Azamgarh, U.P. be transferred to the Additional Chief Metropolita21n Court, Bandra Mumbai, Maharashtra.
</p><p>The Transfer Petition is allowed accordingly.
</p><p>Petition allowed
