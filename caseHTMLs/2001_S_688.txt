<div><h3>State of Haryana and Anr v Naresh Kumar
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>19 March 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A..No.6641/2000
</p><p>The order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that the High Court ought to have entertained the writ petition and decided the question as to whether the appellants could be regarded as an "industry" within the meaning of the Industrial Disputes Act.
</p><p>2.  For the aforesaid reason, this appeal is allowed, the impugned order of the High Court is set aside and the case is remanded to the High Court for a fresh decision in accordance with law.
</p><p>Appeal allowed.
</p></div>
