<div><h3>M.D., U.P.Co-Op.Sugar Factory Fed.Ltd and Anr v G.K. Gupta and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>4 December 2000
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8019/2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  For the reasons recorded in Civil Appeal arising out of SLP (C) No. 18575/1999 titled State of U.P. and Ors.Vs.Karuna Jaiswal decided on 21.2.2000, this appeal is allowed on the same terms and the impugned order passed by the High Court stands modified accordingly.
</p><p>2. The order of the High Court for payment of full salary during the suspension period is set aside. The respondent would be entitled to payment of suspension allowance as per the Rules.
</p><p>3.  The appeal is disposed of accordingly. There will be no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of.
</p></div>
