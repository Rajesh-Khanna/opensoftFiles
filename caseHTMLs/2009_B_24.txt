<div><h3>South Central Railway Emp. C. C. S. E. U. v B. Yashodabai and others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>12 February 2009
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No(s). 7130 of 2002
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  Mr. A.Subba Rao, learned counsel for the appellant has relied on the judgment of this Court in Civil Appeal No.4343 of 1998 dated 13.01.1998 in the case of South Central Railway Employees Co-operative Credit Society Employees Union Secunderabad vs. Registrar of Co-operative Societies <a href="/case/<a href="/case/<a href="/case/1998 Indlaw SC 13">1998 Indlaw SC 13</a>6"><a href="/case/1998 Indlaw SC 13">1998 Indlaw SC 13</a>6</a>3"><a href="/case/<a href="/case/1998 Indlaw SC 13">1998 Indlaw SC 13</a>6"><a href="/case/1998 Indlaw SC 13">1998 Indlaw SC 13</a>6</a>3</a>.
</p><p>2.  On the other hand, Mr. C.N.Sree Kumar, learned counsel for the respondents has submitted that the aforesaid judgment requires re-consideration in view of the facts mentioned in the impugned judgment of the High Court dated 14th August, 2002 which has also considered the aforesaid decision of this Court.
</p><p>3. Under these circumstances, we are of the opinion that it would be appropriate if the matter is considered by a larger Bench. Ordered accordingly.
</p><p>4.  Registry is directed to place the papers before the Hon'ble the Chief Justice of India for placing this matter before a larger Bench.
</p><p>Order accordingly.
