<div><h3>State of Orissa and others v Upal Krishna Redy and others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>18 April 1994
</b><p>
</p><p>C.As. Nos. 2541-42 of 1994
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Mr Sunil Dogra, learned counsel appears for the respondents.
</p><p>1.  Heard parties. The only thing that needs to be clarified is that the respondent workmen who were directed to be paid the same wages as the regular employees would be placed only on the minimum of the pay scales which is prescribed for the regular employees belonging to the same categories.
</p><p>2. The impugned order of the High Court is modified accordingly and the appeal is allowed to the above extent. There will be no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeal allowed.
</p></div>
