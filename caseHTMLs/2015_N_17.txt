<div><h3>Nanuram Mohanlal Varma v Divisional Controller
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>10 December 2015
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i> Anil R. Dave, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>Civil Appeal Nos. 8300-8301 of 2010
</p><p>(Non-Reportable)
</p><p>1. Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>2. Looking at the peculiar facts of the case, we direct that 50% of the principal amount paid to the appellant by way of back wages shall be returned to the respondent-employer within four weeks from today.
</p><p>3. It is clarified that no interest on the amount paid shall be returned. If the amount is not repaid as directed hereinabove, the amount shall be returned with 6% interest p.a. thereon from the date the amount was paid.
</p><p>4. The appeals are disposed of as allowed to the above extent with no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeals disposed of
</p></div>
