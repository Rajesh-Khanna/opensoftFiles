<div><h3>Bhagwan Shri Bhagwan v State of Haryana
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>30 July 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 813/2001
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>Heard learned counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  Without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, it appears to us that it is a fit case in which the appellant, who has been in custody since November 18, 1998, be released on bail pending trial.
</p><p>2. We, accordingly, direct the learned Sessions Judge to release the appellant on bail on furnishing bail bond in the sum of Rs. 10, 000/- with two sureties of the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Sessions Judge. The appeal is disposed of.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of.
</p></div>
