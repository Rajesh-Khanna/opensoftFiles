<div><h3>Collector of Customs v Shrishma Fine Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals (Karnataka)
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>17 December 1996
</b><p>
</p><p>C.As. Nos. 1954-89 of 1990
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1. The respondents imported Phenol USP Grade and claimed the benefit of an exemption notification issued under the provisions of Section 25 of the <a href="/acts/Customs Act, 1962">Customs Act, 1962</a>.
</p><p>2. The exemption therein applied, inter alia, to
</p><p>"pharmaceutical chemicals, i.e., chemicals having prophylactic or therapeutic value and used solely or predominantly as drugs, not elsewhere specified"
</p><p>3. The appeals are dismissed, with no order as to costs.
</p><p>Appeals dismissed.
</p></div>
