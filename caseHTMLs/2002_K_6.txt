<div><h3>K. L. Kunjappan v Rafeeque and Another
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>12 August 2002
</b><p>
</p><p>Cr.A. No. 821 of 2002 (Arising out of S.L.P. (Cr.) No. 1665 of 2002).
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Special leave granted.
</p><p>1.  It is stated by the learned counsel for the respondent that the money due under the cheque has already been received by the respondent.
</p><p>2. In this view of the matter, the sentence awarded is reduced to the period already undergone.
</p><p>This appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of.
