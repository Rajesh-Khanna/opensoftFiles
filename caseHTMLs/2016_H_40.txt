<div><h3>Haryana Vidhyut Prasaran Nigam Limited v Haryana Electricity Regulatory Commission and another
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>14 December 2016
</b><p>The Judgment was delivered by :<i> Kurian Joseph, J.
</i></p><p>
</p><p>Civil Appeal No. 12324 of 2016 [@Civil Appeal D. No. 32409 of 2016]
</p><p>(Non-Reportable)
</p><p>1. Delay condoned.
</p><p>2. It is seen that the APTEL has left the issue regarding impact on tariff under Section 63 of the <a href="/acts/Electricity Act, 2003">Electricity Act, 2003</a> to be decided by the Arbitral Tribunal.
</p><p>3. We make it clear that the Tribunal will take an independent decision, uninfluenced by any of the observations and findings in the impugned Judgment.
</p><p>4. The Arbitral Tribunal is directed to decide the impact on tariff as a preliminary issue.
</p><p>5. In view of the above, the appeal is disposed of. No costs.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of
