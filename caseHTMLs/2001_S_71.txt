<div><h3>Rajkishore Sahoo and Anr v Sachidananda Mishra and Anr
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>5 November 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.5650/2000
</p><p>The order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>Delay condoned. Application for substitution is allowed.
</p><p>Leave granted.
</p><p>1.  In this matter notice was issued to the respondents indicating as to why the matter should not be disposed of by granting injunction only in respect of Schedule'A' portion of the property pending disposal of the suit.
</p><p>2.  Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the respondents states that they have no objection for this course.
</p><p>The appeal shall stand disposed of in the terms stated above.
</p><p>Appeal disposed of.
