<div><h3>Jaspal and Others v State of Haryana and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>17 September 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.15270/2001
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Learned counsel submits that a Full Bench decision of the High Court of Punjab and Haryana in Jai Singh's case has been set aside by this Court and the matter is remitted to the High Court. If the High Court takes a view, the petitioner may also be allowed to take advantage of the same.
</p><p>2. He submits that if this special leave petition is dismissed now, he will be debarred from availing himself of the said benefit subsequently. Issue notice. The petitioner is permitted to serve notice on the Standing Counsel for the State of Haryana.
</p><p>Order accordingly.
</p></div>
