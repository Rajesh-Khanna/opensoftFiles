<div><h3>Ashish Sahkari Grih Nirman Samiti v State of Bihar and Others
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>8 October 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>I.A. No. 1-8 in S.L.P.(C)...(CC.7191/2001)
</p><p>The order of the court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  Mr. Gopal Subramanium, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioners submits that he will file the particulars of the members of the petitioner society who have put up pucca construction in the lands in question and whose houses are likely to be demolished by the impugned order of the High Court.
</p><p>2. He is also directed to give the necessary particulars of the area and date of construction within four weeks. Objections, if any, by respondent No. 5 shall be filed within two weeks thereafter.
</p><p>3.  In the meanwhile, there shall be no demolition of existing structures in the lands in question.
</p><p>List after six weeks.
</p><p>Order accordingly.
</p></div>
