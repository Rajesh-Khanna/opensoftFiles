<div><h3>Commissioner of Income Tax v Krupp Industries India Limited
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>9 August 1999
</b><p>
</p><p>C.A. No. 4424 of 1999 (From the judgment and order Dt. 27 March 1998 of the Bombay High Court in ITA No. 54 of 1998).
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>Delay condoned.
</p><p>Special leave granted.
</p><p>We have heard the counsel for the parties.
</p><p>1.  In our opinion, the question of law definitely arose in this case, we, therefore, allow this appeal, direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following question of law to the High Court :
</p><p>"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was right in law in holding that the payment on account of technical know-how amounting to Rs. 72, 91, 580 is allowable deduction as revenue expenditure under the IT Act, 1961 ?"
</p><p>Appeal allowed.
</p></div>
