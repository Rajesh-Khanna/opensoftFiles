<div><h3>Babujan Tudu and Others v State of Bihar
</h3><p>Supreme Court of India
</p><b>1 August 2001
</b><p>
</p><p>Review Petition(Cr.) No. 698/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 3525/2000
</p><p>The Order of the Court was as follows:
</p><p>1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 195 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no satisfactory explanation. Even on merits, we do not find a case for our interference.
</p><p>2. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
</p><p>Review dismissed.
